How Can RepoHouse Help Me Manage My Assets?

RepoHouse will regularly update you on current market activities to enable you make informed investment decisions. Our investment advisors are always a call away.

What Is Your Minimum Investment Value?

RepoHouse, through its Structured Investment Plan, has investment opportunities from N100,000. Please note that specific products have their own minimum investment amount:

Stockbroking – ₦1,000,000.00 in cash, certificates or transfer of stocks

Fixed Income – ₦1,000,000.00 only in cash

Separately Managed Accounts – ₦5,000,000.00 in cash or transfer of stocks

Structured Investment Plans – ₦100,000.00 in cash and subsequent investments of ₦50,000.00

What are the Requirements for Opening an Account with RepoHouse?

Basic Requirements:

  • Duly Completed Account Opening form
  • A valid government issued ID (International Passport, Driver’s License, National Identity Card or National Voter’s Card). Means of Identification for foreign clients have to be notarized
  • Proof of Address/Utility Bill (not longer than 3 months)  Passport Photograph
  • Signed Brokerage Agreement (for stockbroking clients only)
  • Signed Investor Profile Questionnaire and Portfolio Management Agreement (for portfolio management clients only)
  • Minimum initial deposit

    • Are My Investments Secure with RepoHouse?

    RepoHouse is fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission and the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) to carryout Asset Management and related services. In addition, RepoHouse ways employs first class risk management practices, corporate governance and we always adhere to client’s investment policies. RepoHouse guarantees the principal and interest for all fixed rate tenured investments.
