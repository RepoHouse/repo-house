Strategic Planning

Strategic planning is one our our focal organizational professional activity that allows us to set priorities, focus energy and resources, strengthen operations, ensure that our employees and other stakeholders are working toward common goals, establish agreement around intended outcomes/results, and assess and adjust our client's direction in response to a changing environment. Embarking on these steps produces fundamental decisions and actions that shape and guide what investments best suit our clients with a focus set on the future. Our planning strategies successfully articulate not only where our clients are headed and the actions needed to make progress, but also how to know if the steps they decide to follow will be successful.

We specialize in creating specific investment strategies, implementing them, and evaluating the results of executing the plan, in regard to a clients overall long-term goals or desires.

Investment Banking

At RepoHouse we seek to provide customized solutions to our clients based on our understanding of their strategic objectives. Our focus is to grow with our clients, build relationships based on trust and show developed competence to support businesses at different points in their lifecycles including navigating cyclical downturns.
We cater to clients across diverse sectors and provide expert advice to both large and mid-sized companies as well as government agencies both locally and across the West African region.

We aim to be a long term, trusted adviser and financier to our clients.

We apply ingenuity and deep market knowledge in exploring the full spectrum of financing alternatives in raising capital for our clients. Our investor relationships cover the full spectrum of the private capital markets including private equity funds, pension funds, insurers, strategic investors, fund of funds, investment trusts, sovereign wealth funds and family offices.

Asset Management (Investment Management)

We are experts in the handling of our clients financial assets and other investments performing activites beyond the scope of just buying and selling assets. These activities include devising short- or long-term strategies for acquiring and disposing of portfolio holdings. It can also include banking, budgeting, and tax services and duties, as well.

We are experts of managing client holdings within an investment portfolio, and the trading them to achieve specific investment objectives.

Our management services include asset allocation, financial statement analysis, stock selection, monitoring of existing investments, and portfolio strategy and implementation. Premium services include financial planning and advising services, not only overseeing a client's portfolio but coordinating it with other assets and life goals. pOur Professional managers deal with a variety of different securities and financial assets, including bonds, equities, commodities, and real estate.
Other optional assets that we can manage include assets such as precious metals, commodities, and artwork. Our professionals can help clients align investments to match retirement and estate planning as well as asset distribution.
